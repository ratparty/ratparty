Location

We are located at the docks of Bahia Honda State Park, mile marker 37. Just 40 minutes from Key West, 20 minutes from Marathon, and 2 1/2 hours from Miami. There are easy flights into Key West or Miami, and a variety of hotels and motels for your comfort. You can’t go wrong in the fabulous Florida Keys.

What to Bring

Food, drinks and sunscreen.

About Us
